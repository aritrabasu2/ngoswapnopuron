About Us

At Swapnopuron, we prioritize the holistic development of children. Swapnopuron Welfare Society is a non-profit organization dedicated to transforming the lives of underprivileged children and communities. Founded with a vision of creating a world where every child has access to quality education, healthcare, and a nurturing environment, we have been working tirelessly to uplift the less fortunate sections of Sundarbans since our establishment. Join us in our mission to create a world where every child can dream, thrive, and fulfill their potential. Together, we can make a lasting impact and build a brighter future for generations to come. Swapnopuron Welfare Society - Illuminating Lives, Inspiring Dreams.
The foundation of Swapnopuron Welfare Society (SWS) can be traced back to 2012 when Satarupa Majumder, a passionate teacher from Kolkata, visited Hingalganj in the Sundarbans. Witnessing the lack of educational opportunities and the struggles faced by underprivileged children, Satarupa's dream to bring education to this remote region was ignited. With unwavering determination, she established SWS, one of the first English-medium schools in the Sundarbans. Over the years, SWS has impacted over 1700 children through its five centers, providing quality education, healthcare, and a nurturing environment. Satarupa's infectious determination and tireless hard work have made Swapnopuron a beacon of hope in the community.

Our Mission

Women's Empowerment

Women empowerment refers to the process of granting women the tools, opportunities, and resources necessary to realize their full potential, exercise their rights, and participate actively in all spheres of life. It is a transformative movement aimed at addressing gender inequalities and fostering an inclusive society that recognizes and values the contributions of women. Women empowerment is not merely an individual endeavor but a collective responsibility that brings about positive social, economic, and political change.

Child protection

Child protection refers to the measures and actions taken to ensure the safety, well-being, and rights of children. It encompasses a broad range of initiatives aimed at preventing and responding to various forms of abuse, neglect, exploitation, and violence inflicted upon children. Child protection frameworks and policies are essential for creating a safe and nurturing environment that allows children to grow, develop, and reach their full potential.

Education

Education is the process of acquiring knowledge, skills, values, and attitudes through various methods such as teaching, training, or research. It is a lifelong process that begins at an early age and continues throughout a person's life. Education plays a crucial role in personal and societal development, empowering individuals and contributing to the progress of communities and nations. Formal education typically takes place in educational institutions such as schools, colleges, and universities. It follows a structured curriculum and is often guided by teachers or instructors. Formal education systems vary across countries but generally include primary education, secondary education, and higher education.
